The funny thing about taking journeys and encountering unexopected trails, you get to learn how to forgo the trail altogether and forge your own. I started this particular part of my website with the idea that I'd take Python code from a book and just plant it on a website. Guess, what? Coding isnt that easy. Its not that hard, but still, not that easy. There is a process. A, LEARNING PROCESS, one might say. Full of stress and confusion and unintelligable noises. So I determined that this exercise in futility wouldnt be about doing whats simplest or most efficient, but instead be about learning how to conquer questions I didnt know I'd need to ask. It would simply be about HOW I conquered the problem.
